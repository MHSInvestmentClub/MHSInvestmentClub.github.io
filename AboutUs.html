About Us


Investment club is a club that is dedicated to educating high school students all about finances and managing their money. We place a great emphasis on securities and how you can use your money to invest in different entities to make more money. With this knowledge, we enter students into the YIS stock pitch competition, a presentational skills competition and we also have an ongoing stock simulator competition in which students are allotted a portfolio with a specified amount of cash that they have to manage.
